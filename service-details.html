High precision and detail

With over three decades in the industry, we offer specialized white metal bearing lining and repair service along with the manufacture of new white metal lined bearings.

Our Specialization

White metal bearings are manufactured for a diverse range of industries Power Plants (Steam and hydro turbines), Fertilizer Plants, Steel Plants, Paper Mill, Textile Mills, Sugar Mill and Distilleries for their various critical rotating machinery like Turbines, Pumps, Compressors, Gearboxes, Generators etc. Our range of repair and manufacture includes Plain Journal bearing, Journal cum Thrust bearing, Thrust Pads, Tilting pads journal bearings, and a variety of custom jobs as per customer-specific requirements.

We have equipped our workshop (Unit 1 & Unit 2) with a range of machinery and skilled committed staff incorporating the following facilities amongst others which are being upgraded continuously.

  • Centrifugal Casting
  • Conventional Gravity Casting
  • Ultrasonic Testing as per ISO 4386 - 1
  • Dye Penetrant Testing
  • Bond Testing (with Bond Meter)

Our rigorous in-house testing ensures the dependability of our products.
