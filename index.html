What We Do

Following processes and techniques which have been implemented and improvised over a span of the last 30 years, we have developed the expertise to dismantle, inspect, overhaul, repair, recondition and assemble critical rotating machines. Supporting the repair projects, we also offer services including highly accurate Dynamic Balancing, surface treatment processes of Sand-Blasting, Grit-Blasting and Thermal Spray Coating Process.
