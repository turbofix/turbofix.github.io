Premier Engineering Company – Turbofix Engineers & Consultants

Critical Rotating Machinery Repair Specialists

We provide comprehensive repair services for Boiler Feed Pumps, Dynamic Balancing of critical rotating machinery, Steam Turbines, Sand Blasting, Shafts, Decanter Screws, Runners, other critical rotating machinery like Hammer Mills, Governor and much more. We also manufacture Plain Journal and Thrust bearings, Multi Lobe-Profiled bearings, tilting pad bearings, and guide bearings for clients all over the world, undertaking the operations at our workshops in Chandigarh and Punjab.

About Our Company

We are maintenance specialists who infuse life in your machines, which go on to serve you trouble free well beyond their expected life span. Monitoring every project with utmost detail, concentrating on delivering the highest quality , value and saving downtime remains our aim. Leading the team forward with years of experience and expertise is Mr. Inderjit Singh, who has a history of executing successful projects worldwide including projects with global pump and turbine manufacturers like M/s KSB, M/s KKK and M/s Blohm & Voss, Germany.

Services We Provide

Following processes and techniques which have been implemented and improvised over a span of the last 30 years, we have developed the expertise to provide specialized services with intricate detail.

Steam Turbines

With years of rich experience in the field of Steam Turbines, we undertake repair, replacement of parts, and complete overhaul of both power and industrial steam turbines, supported by extensive workshop facilities and an experienced on-site team.

White Metal Bearings

We specialize in the repair as well as manufacturing of white metal bearings of all sizes and designs. Maintaining the highest standards of workmanship and equipped with the best-in-class equipment, we ensure high quality & timely delivery.

Boiler Feed Pumps

We offer repair services for single as well as multistage boiler feed pumps. Our workshops are well equipped with varied resources od Pump Repair. Afrer thorough analysis our technicians offer & execute the most viable and cost effective solutions.

Dynamic Balancing

We undertake dynamic balancing of diffrent kinds of rotating machines/components and have developed a reputation of delivering the highest levels of accuracy. We use SCHENCK Dynamic balancing machines, operated by highly trained personell.
