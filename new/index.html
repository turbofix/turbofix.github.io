Premier Engineering Company

Turbofix Engineers & Consultants

We provide comprehensive repair services for Decanter Screws, Boiler Feed Pumps, Dynamic Balancing of critical rotating machinery, Steam Turbines, Sand Blasting, Shafts, Runners, other critical rotating machinery like Hammer Mills, Governor and much more. We also manufacture Plain Journal and Thrust bearings, Multi Lobe-Profiled bearings, tilting pad bearings, and guide bearings for clients all over the world, undertaking the operations at our workshops in Chandigarh and Punjab.

About Our Company

We are maintenance specialists who infuse life in your machines, which go on to serve you trouble free well beyond their expected life span. Monitoring every project with utmost detail, concentrating on delivering the highest quality , value and saving downtime remains our aim. Leading the team forward with years of experience and expertise is Mr. Inderjit Singh, who has a history of executing successful projects worldwide including projects with global pump and turbine manufacturers like M/s KSB, M/s KKK and M/s Blohm & Voss, Germany.
